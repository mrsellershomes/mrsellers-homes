Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026 · Last Updated: May 20, 2026

1. Who We Are

Mr. Sellers Homes is the public brand of Tyler Sellers, a licensed real estate agent operating under RE/MAX Signature Homes in Bergen County, New Jersey. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you interact with our website (mrsellers.homes) or services, how we use it, and the choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our website or services, we may collect:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number
  • Property preferences — areas of interest, budget, timeline, buyer/seller intent, agency representation status
  • Open house attendance — information you provide on the sign-in form at our in-person open houses
  • Communications — messages you send us via our web forms, text, email, or phone
  • Site analytics — anonymized usage data via Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity (page views, clicks, session duration); no personally identifying analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and follow up about properties
  • Send showing confirmations, listing updates, and closing logistics
  • Coordinate appointments, viewings, and other in-person meetings
  • Provide market reports and neighborhood information when requested
  • Improve our website and the services we offer

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate (e.g., our CRM, email and SMS delivery providers, cloud hosting and analytics platforms)
  • Other professionals on your transactions — attorneys, lenders, title companies, inspectors, co-brokering agents — only when relevant to closing your specific deal
  • Authorities, if legally required (subpoena, court order, fraud prevention, or to enforce our terms)
SMS-specific carve-out All of the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

5. SMS / Text Messaging

When you opt in to text messages from us — via a web form, the in-person open house sign-in sheet, or by initiating a text conversation with our published number — we may send you transactional and customer-care messages, including showing confirmations and reminders, property listing updates and price changes, document signature reminders, closing date logistics, and post-meeting follow-ups.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your engagement. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message; you can request help by replying HELP. Opting out applies to non-emergency, non-transactional messaging.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our site uses cookies and similar technologies for analytics and performance (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity). These help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; doing so may affect some site features.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You may, at any time:

  • Opt out of SMS by replying STOP to any text we send you
  • Opt out of email by clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email
  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to update or delete your information — email tyler@mrsellers.homes

Depending on where you live (for example, California, Colorado, or other states with consumer privacy laws), you may have additional rights including the right to know what data we hold, to request deletion, or to opt out of certain processing. We honor these requests without discrimination — just send us an email.

8. Data Retention

We keep your information while you are an active client or prospect and for a reasonable period afterward for follow-up and legal recordkeeping — typically up to seven (7) years for transaction records, as required by New Jersey real estate regulations.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted with a new effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
