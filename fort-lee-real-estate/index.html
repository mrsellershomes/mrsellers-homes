What this means right now

Fort Lee is sitting in a balanced spot right now. The market here is condo-heavy, and that pulls the median sale price down to about $390K because 40 of last month’s 47 closings were condos. Inventory is up about 18% from a year ago and months of supply is around 5, which means buyers have a little breathing room and do not have to write panicked offers anymore.
