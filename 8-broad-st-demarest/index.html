The Home

Pure Luxury on the East Hill

Enter into pure luxury on a quiet dead-end street in the East Hill of Demarest. Minutes to New York City via the George Washington Bridge and moments from nearby parks, this new-construction modern colonial offers 4,140 square feet above grade plus 1,510 square feet of finished lower-level space on a 0.319-acre lot. With hundreds of thousands in post-construction upgrades, the home features a variance-approved extra-wide driveway leading to a 3-car garage, stone-accented exterior, and Andersen windows throughout. Walnut-stained oak floors anchor the main level, where a chef's kitchen showcases Statuario quartz, Sub-Zero, Wolf, and a Cove dishwasher alongside a walk-in pantry. Every plumbing fixture is House of Rohl — burnished gold in the kitchen, polished chrome in each bath. A gas fireplace, kitchen island, paver patio, and fully fenced yard make entertaining effortless, while a first-floor junior suite offers multigenerational flexibility. Upstairs, the primary suite features dual walk-in closets, cove lighting, motorized shades, and a spa bath, joined by two junior suites, a fifth bedroom, and a hall bath. The radiant-heated lower level adds a rec room, home gym, wet-bar rough-in, bonus room, and a full bath.

Premium appliances, designer fixtures, smart-home infrastructure, and the expanded driveway were selected and installed by the owner — not standard builder options.
