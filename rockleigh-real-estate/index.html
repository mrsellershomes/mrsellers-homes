What this means right now

Rockleigh's market right now is defined by extreme scarcity. Over the last eight months, only one single-family home sold, a $2,830,000 property that sat for 30 days on market before closing at asking price. That single data point tells you everything you need to know: there is almost no inventory, and when a home does appear, it is priced to sell at value rather than above it. This isn't a hot market. It's a market where homes almost never come available. If you are looking in Rockleigh, you are competing for information and early access far more than you are competing against other buyers at the negotiating table. Work with someone who knows the township well and can alert you the moment a listing hits. Pricing your own home competitively matters enormously here because buyers have nowhere else to go, but they also will not overpay for the privilege of buying into a town with virtually no turnover.
